Accessibility Statement

Last updated: November 2025

Lugvia Movers is committed to providing an accessible experience for all customers, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Measures to support accessibility

  • Semantic HTML structure, ARIA labels where appropriate
  • Keyboard navigable menus and interactive components
  • Color contrast aligned with WCAG recommendations
  • Responsive layouts and scalable text for mobile and desktop
  • Continuous improvements based on user feedback

Feedback and assistance

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our website or services, please contact us so we can help.

  • Phone: +1 (646) 719-7124
  • Email: support@lugvia.com
  • Contact form: Request assistance

Compatibility and standards

We aim to support modern browsers on desktop and mobile. Some features may depend on JavaScript; we provide graceful fallbacks wherever possible. Our site is tested against WCAG 2.1 AA success criteria and we continually monitor for regressions.

Ongoing improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We review new pages and features to ensure they meet our standards, and we welcome feedback to improve.
